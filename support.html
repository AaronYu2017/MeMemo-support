About MeMemo

MeMemo is a personal life timeline app that helps you record every year, month, week, and day of your life.

From a lifetime overview to a single day, your story unfolds naturally through time.

FAQ

Where is my data stored?
All data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to your data.

Do I need an account?
No. MeMemo does not require registration or login.

Can I sync across devices?
Yes. If you enable iCloud, your data syncs automatically across devices signed in to the same Apple ID.
