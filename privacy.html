Overview

MeMemo ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal life recording app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

MeMemo does not collect, transmit, or store any user data on our servers. We do not have servers. All data you create in MeMemo stays entirely under your control.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in two places, both controlled by you:

  • On your device: All records are saved locally on your iPhone or iPad.
  • In your iCloud: If you enable iCloud sync, your data is backed up to your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit service. This data is associated with your Apple ID and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

MeMemo does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service used is Apple's iCloud/CloudKit for optional data sync, which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third party. We cannot access your data because it is stored only on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

Children's Privacy

MeMemo does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not require registration or personal information to use.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by deleting content within the app or by deleting the app from your device. iCloud data can be managed through your device's iCloud settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
